# Takardar Albarkatun Tsarin Mythras Imperative

---
## Gabatarwa

_Mythras Imperative_ wani nau'i ne mai buɗaɗɗe, mai lasisin ORC, na tsarin dokokin _Mythras_. Wannan takarda tana ba da cikakken tsarin dokoki, amma ba mai rufe komai ba, wanda ya haɗa da ƙirƙirar hali, ƙwarewa, dokoki don wasu yanayi na yau da kullun, faɗa, da sihiri – fiye da isa don fara duka 'yan wasa da Masu Gudanar da Wasanni (Games Masters), da kuma samar da tushe mai ƙarfi don ci gaba. _Mythras Imperative_ yana dacewa da manyan dokokin Mythras, da sauran tsarin wasanni na kashi dari (percentile gaming systems) masu lasisin ORC. Littafinsa na abokin tafiya, _Classic Fantasy Imperative_, shima yana dacewa gaba ɗaya kuma an sake shi ƙarƙashin ORC, ma'ana za a iya haɗa Imperatives guda biyu cikin sauƙi.

Fassarorin da ba na Turanci ba ana yin su ta hanyar AI. Ana maraba da gudummawar al'umma!

## Lasisi na ORC

_Mythras Imperative_ an buga shi ta amfani da Lasisin ORC. Lasisin ORC yana ba da hanya ga mahaliccin wasa don raba injiniyoyin wasan su a fili kuma yana ba da damar ga masu amfani na gaba su yi amfani da su kyauta, su gyara, da kuma sake raba gyare-gyaren waɗannan injiniyoyin wasan.

Abin da aka raba a fili ƙarƙashin ORC an san shi da _**Kayan Lasisi**_, kuma ya haɗa da manyan abubuwan aiki na wasan, kamar tubalan ƙididdiga (stat blocks), dokokin wasa, halayen hali, da hanyoyi da tsarin da ke tattare da wasan, da kuma duk wani abu da mai lasisin ke son rabawa a fili. Lasisin yana da faɗi kuma yana ba ku damar amfani da Kayan Lasisi a duniya baki ɗaya dangane da littattafan bugawa, wasannin bidiyo, podcasts, AI, ko duk wata fasaha da za ta iya wanzuwa ko a ƙirƙira a nan gaba. Yawancin abubuwan da aka samu a cikin _Mythras Imperative_ sun haɗa da Kayan Lasisi.

Abin da _ba_ a raba ƙarƙashin ORC an san shi da _**Kayan Tanadi**_ kuma ya haɗa da alamun kasuwanci, tarihin duniya, zane-zanen labari (story arcs), halaye na musamman, da fasahar gani. Mun lissafta Kayan Tanadi a cikin Sanarwar ORC, a ƙasa.

Ƙarƙashin Lasisin ORC za ku iya amfani, raba, daidaitawa, da ginawa akan Kayan Lasisi duk yadda kuka ga dama, muddin kun ba da sanarwa mai kyau a cikin aikin ku da aka buga. Wannan sanarwa tana ba da daraja ga masu ba da lasisi na farko da gudummawar su ga aikin, haka kuma tana samar da hanya mai sauƙi ga masu amfani na gaba don ba da daraja ga marubucin aikin da sanarwar ta bayyana a ciki. Ta hanyar amfani da kayan da aka ba da lasisi ƙarƙashin ORC, kun yarda ta atomatik don ba da lasisi ga injiniyoyin wasan ku a cikin aikin ku da aka buga ƙarƙashin sharuɗɗa iri ɗaya. Idan kun haɓaka naku Kayan Tanadi, wannan dukiyar ku ce ta musamman.

Idan kuna son amfani da abubuwan da aka samu a cikin ko dai _**Classic Fantasy**_ ko _**Mythras**_, da fatan za ku tuntuɓi The Design Mechanism game da lasisin _Mythras Gateway License_ na daban, wanda ke ba da damar samun Kayan Tanadi, ba tare da biyan kuɗi ba, amma tare da wasu sharuɗɗa game da amincewa da bada daraja.

## Sababbi ga d100?

_Mythras Imperative_ tsari ne na d100 ko kashi dari. Ana amfani da dice na kashi dari, ko 1d100, don warware manyan ayyuka – amfani da ƙwarewa, faɗa, jefa sihiri, da sauransu. Tsarin jefa-ƙasa ne, ma'ana cewa an bayyana lambar manufa a matsayin kashi dari (misali, Athletics 65%), kuma duka Masu Gudanar da Wasanni (Games Masters) da ɗan wasa suna son jefa dai-dai ko ƙasa da wannan lambar manufa, ta amfani da 1d100, don samun Nasara. Yana da sauƙi a maye gurbin 1d100 da d20 idan an fi so, kuma za a iya raba duk kashi dari da 5 (a zagaye sama) don samun lambar manufa don jefa d20. Athletics 65% zai zama Athletics 13, a cikin misalinmu.

Duk da haka, 1d100 yana ba da sassauci da ma'ana mai yawa wanda ke goyon bayan dukan wasan, don haka shawararmu ita ce a gwada tsarin 1d100 da farko. Yayin da zai iya zama kamar ba a saba gani ba dole ne a jefa _ƙasa_ da wani abu (a yawancin wasanni, mafi girma yawanci shine mafi kyau!), samun lambar manufa da aka bayyana a matsayin kashi dari wanda mutum ke buƙatar jefawa a ciki yana sa ya zama mai sauƙi da bayyane don ganin damar ku ta nasara, kuma yana ba da damar 'dabaru na dice' da yawa, kamar Jefa-Jefa Na Adawa (Opposed Rolls), Jefa Mai Muhimmanci (Criticals) da Kuskure Mai Girma (Fumbles), da wasu abubuwa da za a bincika daga baya a cikin waɗannan dokokin. Wasan kwaikwayo na kashi dari (percentile roleplaying) ya kasance tun daga farkon zamanin wasan kwaikwayo kuma tsari ne na wasa mai ƙarfi tare da injiniya mai mahimmanci wanda aka gwada kuma aka tabbatar da shi mai sassauci, mai fahimta, kuma yana yakan ɓace a bango yayin wasan.

## Sauƙaƙewa

Masu _**Mythras**_ na dogon lokaci za su lura cewa a wasu wurare an sauƙaƙe dokokin _Mythras Imperative_. An yi haka ne domin Masu Gudanar da Wasanni (Games Masters) su iya gabatar da wasan ga sababbin 'yan wasa da sauri ba tare da wata matsala ba.

---
## Zagaye Lambobi da Sakamako

A wasu lokuta za a buƙaci ku raba lambobi – yawanci ƙimar ƙwarewa (kamar don tantance Nasara Mai Muhimmanci (Critical Success), wanda shine 1/10 na ƙimar ƙwarewar). Duk lokacin da sakamakon raba ya haifar da rabi, koyaushe a zagaye sama zuwa cikakken lamba. Don haka, misali, 1/10 na 63% shine 6.3; wannan an zagaye shi zuwa 7.

## Dice da Ake Amfani da Su

_Mythras Imperative_ yana amfani da daidaitaccen saitin dice mai siffofi daban-daban: d4, d6, d8, d10 d12, d20 da d100. Bugu da kari, ana neman dice masu zuwa:

d2: Jefa kowane die. Lambar da ba ta raba biyu (odds) daidai take da 1 kuma lambar da ta raba biyu (evens) daidai take da 2. Ko kuma jefa tsabar kudi.

d3: Jefa die mai fuskoki shida; 1-2 = 1, 3-4 = 2, 5-6 = 3
