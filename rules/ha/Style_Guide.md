# Jagorar Salo (h1)

Yi amfani da kanun h1 (`# Heading Title`) don babban taken babi. Duk kanun hx za su bayyana a gefen shafi a matsayin alamomin shafi.

Idan take ba kanun h1 bane, gefen shafi zai nuna kuskuren maimaitawa wanda ke nuna shafin sannan kanun h2 'take'.

---
## Kanun Sashi (h2)

Yi amfani da kanun h2 (`## Section Heading`) wanda raba (`---`) ke gabata don kowane sabon babban sashi.

### Kanun Sashen Ciki suna ƙara matakin h da 1

Yi amfani da kanun h3 (`### Subsection Heading`) don kowane sabon Sashen Ciki, da kanun h4 (`#### Sub-Subsection Heading`) don kowane sabon karamin sashen ciki.

### Ci gaban h na Sashen Ciki: 2 > 3 > 4

Kanun sashen ciki suna ruɗewa a gefen shafi idan ba a yi amfani da ci gaban h daidai ba.

Alamar kanu ya kamata ya ƙaru da ɗaya ko babu.

h2 sannan h2 ko h3 yana da kyau. h3 sannan h3 ko h4 ma yana da kyau.

### Kurakuran Sashen Ciki

h2 sannan h4 yana haifar da matsaloli. Gefen shafi yana sanya karamin kanun a wani wuri sama da kanun a cikin teburin abubuwan ciki.

### Rage matakan h

Wannan baya zama matsala. misali. h4 zuwa h3 ko h2 yana da kyau.

## Ka'idojin Tsarin Rubutu

Salon da ke gaba sune ka'idodi don waɗannan SRD na _Imperative_ a tsarin Markdown.

### Kaurace, Lankwasa da Masu Rabawa

- Yi amfani da taurari biyu (`**Bold**`) don rubutun **Kaurace**.
- Yi amfani da layin ƙasa (`_Italic_`) don rubutun _Lankwasa_.
  - _lura: Ka guji amfani da taurari ɗaya don Lankwasa!_
- Yi amfani da slash na baya (`\_`) don 'gudu' hali: \_Ba Lankwasa bane\_.
- Yi amfani da ƙa'idoji masu kusurwa (``` `code` ```) don yin `code blocks`.
- Yi amfani da dashes uku (`---`) don saka layin rabawa a kwance.

### Ka'idojin Kaurace & Lankwasa

Yi ƙoƙarin tsayawa ga waɗannan tsarin...

#### Kanun Kaurace

Yi amfani da kanun kaurace don bayanin sihiri, tasiri da sauransu.
- **Sihiri Take:** Bayani.

#### Kanun Lankwasa

Yi amfani da kanun lankwasa don 'kananun jerin' nau'ikan sihiri da sauransu.
- **Sihiri:** Bayani.
  - **_Sihiri Na Musamman-A:_** Bayani.
  - **_Sihiri Na Musamman-B:_** Bayani.

#### Nassoshin Kanun Lankwasa

Yi amfani da kanun lankwasa lokacin da ake ambaton littafi kamar _Mythras Imperative_ ko ka'idojin _Mythras_.

## Mahada

Akwai amfani da yawa don mahada a cikin SRD kuma kaɗan ne kawai ake rufewa anan.

### Tsarin Mahada

Ana yin tsarin mahada da rubutun cikin akwati. Yi amfani da akwatunan murabba'i don rubutun mahada da ake gani, da akwatunan lankwasa don URL ɗin inda za a je: `[Link Text](URL)`.

A cikin waɗannan takardun, mafi yawan mahadar waje da ake amfani da su za su kasance [Mythras Encounter Generator](https://mythras.skoll.xyz).
```
[Mythras Encounter Generator](https://mythras.skoll.xyz)
```

### Mahada na waje vs na ciki

Mahada na waje suna zuwa wasu shafuka ta hanyar intanet kuma za su buƙaci URL na `http://example.com` kuma, ta hanyar tsoho, za su _ko da yaushe_ buɗe a sabon shafin bincike.

Don samun shafuka da yawa suna ɓata takardar da ake karantawa, mahada na ciki da alamomin shafi ana samar da su tare da URLs masu dangantaka da ke nuna fayilolin markdown da ake gani a ma'ajiyar GitHub. Maimakon sabon shafi, waɗannan mahada na ciki za su yi saurin gungurawa zuwa sashin da aka haɗa.

#### Misalan mahada na ciki

- **Mahadar Babi:** Waɗannan suna nuni zuwa farkon babban sashi (h1). misali. babi na [Yaƙi](0005_Combat.md).

  - Tsarin mahada: `[Combat](0005_Combat.md)`

- **Mahadar Sashen Ciki:** Waɗannan suna nuni zuwa wani kanu a cikin babi da ke ambaton takardar kamar yadda aka gani a sama amma yana ƙara `?id=` sannan nan take kanun da za a je. misali. [Harin Tsalle](0005_Combat.md?id=leaping-attacks)

  - Tsarin mahada: `[Leaping Attacks](0005_Combat.md?id=leaping-attacks)`

#### Mahada na waje zuwa sassan ciki

Za a iya samun lokacin da ake buƙatar ambaton wani sanannen sashi na takardar waje, misali, takamaiman tebur a cikin wani SRD. Tsarin yana daidai da mahada na sashen ciki, kamar yadda aka gani a sama, kawai amfani da URL na HTTP na waje.

A matsayin misali, ga teburin [Ma'aunin Imperial zuwa Metric](https://cfi-srd.mythras.net/#/Appendix_B_Conversion_Tables?id=imperial-weights-and-measures-to-metric-conversion) daga [Classic Fantasy Imperative SRD](https://cfi-srd.mythras.net/).

## Jerin Sihiri da Iko

Fara 'sashi mai tsabta' da kanun h2 don jerin sihiri, iko da sauransu; sannan h3 don kowane abu a cikin jerin.

Sihiri da iko galibi ana gabatar da su a cikin jerin da aka haɗe ta nau'in sihiri (Sihiri na Al'ada, Theism, da sauransu) amma idan jerin yana buƙatar nasa sassan ciki, kamar makaranta ko kwarewar al'ada, ƙara matakin kanu daidai. Misali, h2 don Jerin Mu'ujizai na Theism, h3 don Al'ada: Mithras, da h4 don kowane mu'ujiza na al'adar Mithras.

Musamman a cikin yanayin sihiri, amma a kowane bayanin da ke amfani da halayen kalmomi masu mahimmanci, lissafta su a cikin rubutu mai lankwasa.

Rubutun bayani da ka'idodi na musamman suna amfani da rubutu na yau da kullun.

### Tsarin Jerin Sihiri

```
## h2 List name

### List item

_Attributes_

Description text.
```

## Gyaran Al'umma

### Kurakuren Rubutu da Ruɗani

Ya kamata a rage gyare-gyare don 'tsaftace' rubutun marubutan zuwa mafi ƙaranci amma ya kamata a canza kurakuren rubutu da sauran kurakurai kai tsaye ba tare da wata alama ba.

### Bangon Bayanin Gyara

Inda wani ka'ida ba ta da tabbas, ba ta cika ba, ko kuma kuskure ce kawai, ƙara bayanin Gyaran Al'umma mai rugujewa.

Bayyana matsalar a cikin lankwasa kawai amfani da kaurace lankwasa don taken matsalar - _babu kanun h(x)!_ - don kada a haifar da shigarwa mai ruɗani a gefen shafi.

Idan an ƙara rubutun maye gurbin, sanya shi a cikin alamar rubutu biyu a cikin kaurace lankwasa, wanda aka gabatar da "_Gyaran Al'umma:_" a cikin lankwasa mai nauyi na yau da kullun.

Ƙarin ka'idodi, idan an buƙata, ya kamata a gabatar da su a cikin rubutu mai nauyi na yau da kullun.

## Gargaɗi Daban-daban

### Layuka marasa komai / Komawar Kwana

A bar layin fanko tsakanin nau'ikan shigarwa daban-daban. Kanu, masu rabawa, teburai da sauransu. Waɗannan ba za su bayyana a fili a cikin kallon karatu ba amma rashinsu na iya haifar da matsalolin tsari.

### Rubutun sassa da yawa mai kauri ko lankwasa

Kowane sashi dole ne a sanya shi a matsayin rubutu mai kauri ko lankwasa. Sabon layi tsakanin sassan zai karya tsarin yana barin alamomin tsari marasa amfani.

### Rubutun kalmomi na Duniya

Ikon da ke komawa baya da abin zargi sun ga ya dace a cire duk rubutun kalmomi na Birtaniya don fifita nau'ikan US da ke da rauni a fili. A kula da kalmomin Birtaniya -OUR: _armour_, _favour_, da sauransu.
